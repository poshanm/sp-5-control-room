Sabhi karmchari kripya dhyaan dein. Aaj ka safety point hai PPE ka sahi upyog. Helmet, safety shoes aur gloves bina pehne kaam karna durghatna ko bulava dena hai. PPE aapki pehli suraksha hai, koi formality nahi. Kripya apna PPE check karke hi kaam shuru karein. Yaad rakhein, suraksha pehle — hamesha surakshit tareeke se kaam karein. Dhanyavaad.
